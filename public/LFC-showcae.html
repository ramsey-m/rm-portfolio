Liverpool Football Club
Website Redesign

Skills

UI/UX, Front-End
Graphic Design

Team

Solo

Tools

Figma
Balsamq

Date

2020

As one of the world's best teams, liverpoolfc.com gets a lot of traffic. But the website could use some redesigning to make it clearer and more visually appealing. After an engrossing all nighter, this is the beginning of my effort.

Before & After

Hero Section

The original website (left) looks cluttered. There are two menu bars, each with several options, sometimes repeated. I incorporated all the menu items into one concise navigation bar. Also in the real website a carousel is used to highlight some of the clubs recent news. I thought this hero section could be better employed as a promotional/branding space for the club and the design could be greatly improved. In this example, I vectorized some player images to align with recent good news. The sponsor section is clear in both. But in the adapted version, logos are centered and given more breathing room.

News Section

The amended news section employs visual hierarchy techniques to make the reading experience more intuitive and pleasant. Readers can also sign up for the club newsletter.
